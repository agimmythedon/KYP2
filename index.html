ABOUT KYP

Know Your Power (KYP) is a movement dedicated to empowering communities, building ethical leadership and creating a better future for all South Africans.

  • Who We Are

    A collective of diverse citizens committed to real, verifiable socio-economic change across all 9 provinces.

  • Mission

    To secure sustainable community wealth structures and combat corruption at all infrastructural levels.

  • Vision

    A unified state where the constitutional power truly resides within informed communities.

  • Values

    Transparency, Ethical Governance, Uncompromised Integrity, and Respect.
