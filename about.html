About KYP

Know Your Power (KYP) is a movement dedicated to empowering communities, building ethical leadership, and creating a better future for all South Africans. We believe every citizen has the power to shape their destiny and the destiny of their nation.

Founded on the principles of accountability, service, and unity, KYP works tirelessly across all nine provinces to ensure that every South African — regardless of background — has a voice and the tools to use it.

Our Mission

To empower every South African citizen with knowledge, tools, and representation — creating communities that are informed, engaged, and capable of demanding ethical leadership and real change.

👁️

Our Vision

A South Africa where every citizen knows their power, exercises their rights, and participates fully in a democratic society that delivers economic justice, safety, and opportunity for all.

⚖️

Our Values

Accountability in leadership, service to communities, unity across all cultures and backgrounds, ethical leadership at every level, and empowerment of every South African citizen.
