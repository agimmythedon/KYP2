Introduction

Know Your Power (KYP) was founded on a simple but powerful idea: that every South African citizen holds immense power — the power to vote, to demand accountability, and to build the future they deserve.

This manifesto represents our covenant with the people of South Africa. It outlines the specific policies, plans, and commitments we will pursue to build a fairer, safer, and more prosperous nation for all.

We believe that good governance is not a privilege — it is a right. And we are committed to delivering it.

Economic Development

A strong economy is the foundation of everything we want to achieve. KYP will implement targeted economic policies that grow the economy, reduce inequality, and create real opportunity for every South African.

Support for small and medium enterprises (SMMEs) through grants and low-interest loans
Attract foreign investment through a stable and transparent regulatory environment
Develop industrial hubs in underdeveloped provinces
Reduce red tape for new businesses and entrepreneurs
Promote township economies and informal sector support

Youth Empowerment

South Africa's youth are not the leaders of tomorrow — they are the leaders of today. KYP commits to investing in young people now, giving them the skills, opportunities, and platforms they need to succeed.

National youth skills development programme in partnership with private sector
Youth entrepreneurship fund with mentorship support
Mental health support programmes for young people in communities
Youth leadership academies in all nine provinces
Internship and work placement guarantee for graduates

Education

Education is the most powerful tool we have to transform South Africa. KYP will fight to ensure every child — regardless of where they are born — has access to quality education that prepares them for life.

Free quality early childhood development (ECD) centres in every community
Fully resourced schools with qualified teachers in all provinces
Digital learning tools and internet access in every school
Increased bursaries and student funding for higher education
Adult literacy and lifelong learning programmes

Job Creation

Unemployment is South Africa's most urgent challenge. KYP will implement bold, targeted job creation strategies that put people to work in every sector of the economy.

Public employment programmes in infrastructure, environment, and community services
Incentives for businesses that hire young and previously unemployed people
Agriculture expansion to create rural employment
Green economy jobs in renewable energy and sustainability
Township business hubs creating local employment

Community Safety

Every South African deserves to feel safe in their community, their school, and their home. KYP will take decisive action to tackle crime, gender-based violence, and the collapse of safety in too many of our communities.

Community policing forums strengthened and properly resourced
Zero tolerance approach to gender-based violence with dedicated courts
SAPS reform and accountability measures to rebuild public trust
Lighting and infrastructure improvements in high-crime areas
Rehabilitation programmes for offenders to reduce recidivism

Good Governance

South Africa needs leadership that is honest, capable, and accountable. KYP will champion good governance at every level of government — from local municipalities to national departments.

Strengthen anti-corruption institutions and protect whistleblowers
Independent auditing of all government contracts and tenders
Publish all public spending online for citizen access
Term limits and performance reviews for public officials
Participatory budgeting — citizens vote on local government spending priorities
